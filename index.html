Online Villagers Chess Opening Book

How to Use

1. Make moves by clicking pieces and moving them to the desired square. Moves are not validated so make sure you play the move you intend!

2. The moves are recorded and checked against an opening database.

3. Rotate the board to view from Black's perspective.

4. Reset the board at any time to restart.

5. Press the undo button to go back to the previous move.
